## Topic

**Black-Scholes Option Pricing & Greeks** — Covers the analytical Black-Scholes formula for pricing European options, computation of option Greeks (Delta, Gamma, Vega), and Monte Carlo simulation methods.

## Key Topics

- Black-Scholes formula for European Call and Put options
- - Option Greeks: Delta, Gamma, Vega, Theta, Rho
  - - Monte Carlo simulation for option pricing
    - - Geometric Brownian Motion (GBM)
      - - Delta and Gamma hedging strategies
        - - Implied volatility
